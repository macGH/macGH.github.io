Blackjack

Should you Hit, Split or Stand?

A command-line version of Blackjack that determines whether you should Hit, Split or Stand.

  • Run blackjack_nightmare.rb to receive a suggested next move based on your given cards, the dealer's top card and the number of decks.

  • Date: February 2016
  • Client: Iron Yard
  • Category: Week 1 Project
